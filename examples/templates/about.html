About Us

We're a team of passionate developers building tools that make the web better.

Our Mission

At Acme Inc., we believe great software starts with great tools. Our mission is to build lightweight, accessible, and performant UI components that developers actually enjoy using. No bloat, no lock-in, just clean code that works.

Founded in 2020, we've grown from a two-person garage operation to a team of 25 engineers, designers, and developer advocates spread across three continents.
